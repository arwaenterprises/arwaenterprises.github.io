How it works

Location-Locked Attendance

1

Define Your Work Zone

Admin drops a pin on the map and sets a radius — from 10 metres to 500 metres. Each department can have unlimited locations (multiple gates, warehouses, sites).

2

Employee Punches In

When the employee taps punch, the app reads their GPS coordinates. If they're inside the geo-fence, punch is accepted. Outside the zone — punch is blocked with a clear message.

3

Location Stored on Record

Every accepted punch stores GPS coordinates alongside the timestamp, photo, and employee ID. Full audit trail for any dispute.
