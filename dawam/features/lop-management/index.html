How it works

Draft → Approve → Lock

1

Supervisor Raises LOP

Supervisor selects employee, date, and reason. Saved as Draft — not yet affecting payroll. Can be edited or deleted at this stage.

2

Admin Reviews & Approves

Admin sees all pending LOPs. Approves or rejects. Approver name and timestamp are stamped on the record permanently.

3

Undo Before Payroll Freeze

Made a mistake? Admin can undo any approved LOP before payroll freeze. Once frozen, records are locked for compliance.

4

Reflected in Billing Report

Approved LOPs automatically adjust the monthly billing report — absent days reduce paid days and salary recalculates.
