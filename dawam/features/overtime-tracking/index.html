How it works

From Hours Worked to SAR Calculated

1

Supervisor Submits OT

For any date, supervisor selects employee and enters OT hours. Each role has a pre-configured hourly OT rate in SAR.

2

SAR Amount Auto-Calculated

Dawam multiplies hours × role OT rate to give the SAR amount instantly. Supervisor sees the total before confirming.

3

Included in Billing Report

All OT entries appear as a dedicated column in the 3PL billing report. Summary shows total OT SAR per client.
