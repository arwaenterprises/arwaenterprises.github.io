Everything You Need to Manage Attendance

Purpose-built for logistics teams, 3PL operators, and contract labour businesses.

🤖

AI Face Recognition

Real-time AI biometric punch with live confidence scoring. Configurable threshold, self-enrollment via secure link, admin approval workflow.

📍

Geo-Fenced Punch

GPS-verified punch blocked outside the work zone. Set 10–500m radius per location. Unlimited locations per department. Works offline too.

📊

3PL Billing Reports

Daily breakdown per laborer — P/H/A/F/NH status, hours, salary (SAR), OT. Export to color-coded Excel or print landscape. Client-ready in minutes.

💰

LOP Management

Full approval workflow: Draft → Supervisor → Admin. Undo before payroll freeze. Approver name and timestamp on every record.

📶

Punch Works Offline

Installable PWA — runs fully offline after first load. Punches stored locally with photos, auto-syncs when internet returns.

⏱️

Overtime Tracking

Submit OT hours per laborer per date. Auto-calculates SAR amount by role rate. Full history and summary included in billing report.

🏢

Multi-Department Roles

Admin: full access. Supervisor: own department only, can raise LOP and submit OT. Punch Terminal: public, no password needed.

📥

Payroll-Ready Exports

Auto-calculate: Salary = (Paid Days ÷ Total Days) × Monthly Salary. Payroll lock freezes dates from edits. Export to CSV or Excel.

Up and Running in 3 Simple Steps

No complex setup. Start tracking attendance from day one.

1

Register Employees

Admin sends a secure one-time enrollment link. Employee scans their own face. Admin reviews and approves the photo before activation.

2

Employee Punches In/Out

Employee scans face at the Punch Terminal (tablet or phone). AI identifies them instantly. GPS verifies they're inside the work zone.

3

Manager Reviews & Exports

Approve LOP corrections, submit overtime, export the 3PL billing report to Excel — salary and OT auto-calculated. Freeze payroll when done.
